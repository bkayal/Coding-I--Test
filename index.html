Malware Milestones


  1. The Creeper Worm


    • Saheb S. Creeper Virus Text. Acessed 2021.

    • In the early 1970s, computers were beginning to be seen more and more throughout daily lives. With this growing accessibility also came new dangers. Viruses, or more broadly, malware. Possibly the first known instance of malware is known as the creeper virus or creeper worm. This malware would copy itself to your computer system, and display the message seen above- "IM THE CREEPER. CATCH ME IF YOU CAN!"
      • It could then move from system to system via the ARPANET (a rudimentary form of the much larger internet we know today), something its creator, Bob Thomas had access to. Bob simply wanted to create a program that was self replicating, and unwittingly gave birth to one of the worst internet phenomena we know today. The modern virus can be found throughout the internet, and is far more detrimental to computer health and your personal well being.
